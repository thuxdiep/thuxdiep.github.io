Kneron Auto Notify Tailgating (ANT)


ANT is a security system that relies on Facial Recognition to recognize familiars registered in the database and triggers Tailgating (where the camera follows one’s movements) if any of the detected faces isn’t recognized. I was part of the Front-end team, where I contributed as a UX/UI Designer and Developer. Please click on "Google Slides" for Full Size Presentation.
