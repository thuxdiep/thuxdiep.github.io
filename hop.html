Hooked on Phonetics - Finding Songs That Fit Your Vocal Range


Hooked on Phonetics (HoP) is a mobile application that allows users to find songs that fit their vocal range using either audio or text input. This is perfect for singing enthusiasts who may need THE perfect song for their performance! I was in charge of designing the UI/UX, while also contributing to researching and testing processes.
