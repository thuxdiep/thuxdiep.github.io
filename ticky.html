Ticky - Mobile Movie Ticketing


Ticky is a mobile application that allows users to quickly and conveniently buy movie tickets online, without any hassles that may come with buying in person. This is a project I had an opportunity to work on during my Google UX Design course on Coursera. I took ownership of the entire project, from ideating solutions to researching, as well as prototyping. Please click on "Google Slides" for Full Size Presentation.
